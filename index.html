Google chromium explained

Now before we begin the comparisons there is one topic I have to explain first and that is what google chromium is. Now most of you have probably never heard of what google chromium is and i'll explain it right now. Google chromium is a software developed by google that most browsers use. In fact there are only two notable browsers in the world that don't use chromium: firefox and safari. Chromium is used by many browsers as it gives two major benefits. 1. Most developers build their websites around this software making them load better and properly on chromium based browsers which have the biggest market share. 2. They give the benefit of having access to the google web store which is the biggest extension store in the world. Now these are some substantial benefits however this gives google the power to affect other browsers as they can issue updates to chromium which gives them massive control over any browsers that use chromium. Since google is a data company their main source of income is ads and collecting user data for capitalization with chromium google may be able to take data from browsers apart from chrome and use it for their own capitalisation purposes that do not align with the users wishes.
